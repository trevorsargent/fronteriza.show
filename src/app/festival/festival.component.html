Co-conceived and wholly organized by la fabulosa, Anaïs Isiria Gurrola, pete took over Portland Playhouse’s courtyard and community space on July 3, 2021 for our ¡Festival de Fronterizas! Our celebration of all things de nuestra gente, we gathered food, artistas, y cultura from gente rooted aquí in Portland.

Local musicians The Lxcals and Mike Firm, Drag Performer Camilo Camellia y DJ Lapaushi shared their time and talents to an appreciative crowd.

Artistas Terry Blas , Virginia Marting, Art Aguilar, Dominique Palafox, y Izá Avalos of poetic bruja botanica shared their wares. Hilary Cantú held an art therapy workshop, magical human and Tarot reader, Selene Torres-Medrano rocked all of our worlds with her accurate and intimate readings.

Our community partners, Portland Through a Latinx Lens y Forward Together shared information about our comunidad in PDX; y finalmente, Rip City Chamoy amazed taste buds and soothed our souls with their excellent chamoy, dulces y fruta enchilados, agua fresca, ceviche y zarandeados.
