Fronteriza vive y respira en la complex personality of La Frontera, borders and borderlands. Told en un español familiar y ingles,

Fronteriza is about finding your home and still having an arduous journey ahead. Llegar nunca es llegar.

Cuenta la historia de encontrar tu casa, hogar, pero todavía teniendo una travesía muy dura adelante.

It’s a story of colonization, immigration, and an attempt to heal from the deep wounds inflicted by our colonial ancestors.

Es una crónica que nos duele porque tiene que ver con la conquista, la migración, y ese sentido que tenemos que no tiene nombre pero nos sigue.

This is our space where we reconcile, only by bearing witness. Veo no veo. Miro no miro.

It was created together, and now again with you. It has been through many iterations.

Es creado con heridas y curas que son ancianas pero también van a durar por más tiempo que nosotras.

Fronteriza offers a series of offerings/retablos.

Inspired by the retablos of Mexico, small devotional paintings, often depicting one moment of divine intervention, as votive offerings left where tragic events have happened,

we offer our aural, visual and theatrical retablos to keep vigil, to make visible those in the borderlands. Es nuestra forma de dar cempasúchil a nuestras tumbas

Ofrecemos estos retablos visuales, llena de alma y teatrales para recordar, lamentar, y mantener vigilia.

The offerings can be found above, all inspired by the global immigration crisis, and told through the lens of Cristi’s experience growing up along the US/MEXICO border.
