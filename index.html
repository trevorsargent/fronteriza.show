About The Show

Galvanized by the current global immigration crisis and PETE (Portland Experimental Theatre Ensemble) member Cristi Milesâ€™ personal experience growing up Mexican/American in El Paso/Juarez, Fronteriza describes the complex personality of borders and borderlands

Told in Spanish and English, Fronteriza is about needing a new home, getting there, and still having an arduous journey ahead. It is a story of colonization, immigration, and how to heal from the deep wounds inflicted by our ancestors. It is a story of how we can reconcile by bearing witness.

Fronteriza is being offered and conceived as a series of events/offerings/retablos. We are inspired by the retablos of Mexico, small devotional paintings, often depicting one moment of divine intervention. Like votive offerings left where tragic events have happened, we offer our theatrical retablos to keep vigil, to make visible those waiting in the borderlands
